pynteractome.layers module¶

class pynteractome.layers.LayersIntegrator(interactome, mendeliome, hpo, prop_depth=0)[source]¶

Bases: object

Integration of the 3 layers that are:

  1. Interactome
  2. OMIM phenotypes
  3. Mendeliome
Parameters:
  • interactome (Interactome) – the interactome
  • mendeliome (MendeliomeParser) – the mendeliome
  • hpo (nx.Graph) – the Human Phenotype Ontology
  • prop_depth (int) – the depth to propagate HPO term \(\rightarrow\) genes associations (see propagate_genes())
Variables:
  • interactome (Interactome) – the interactome
  • mendeliome (MendeliomeParser) – the mendeliome
  • hpo (nx.Graph) – the Human Phenotype Ontology
  • propagation_depth (int) – the current up-propagation depth in the HPO term \(\rightarrow\) OMIM phenotype associations
  • gene2omim (dict) – mapping Entrez gene \(\rightarrow\) OMIM phenotypes
  • omim2hpo (dict) – mapping OMIM phenotype \(\rightarrow\) HPO terms
  • gamma_union (dict) –

    mapping HPO term \(\rightarrow\) Entrez gene s.t.:

    \[\gamma_\cup(t) = \bigcup_{\omega \in \mathcal H_t}\mathcal M^\omega\]
  • gamma_inter (dict) –

    mapping HPO term \(\rightarrow\) Entrez gene s.t.:

    \[\gamma_\cap(t) = \bigcap_{\omega \in \mathcal H_t}\mathcal M^\omega\]
  • omim2gene (dict) – mapping OMIM phenotype \(\rightarrow\) Entrez genes
  • hpo2omim (dict) – mapping HPO term \(\rightarrow\) OMIM phenotypes
  • terms_by_order (list) – list of sets of HPO terms s.t. terms_by_order[i] is the set of all HPO terms of depth i
  • depths (dict) – mapping HPO term \(\rightarrow\) depth (depths[term] == term depth)
