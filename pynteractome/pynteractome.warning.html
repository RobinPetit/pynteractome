pynteractome.warning.set_warning_mode(mode='log')[source]¶

Set the behaviour of warnings. Available parameters:

  • 'log': log the warnings on sys.stdout
  • 'stderr': log the warnings on sys.stderr
  • 'raise': raise a WarningException
  • 'ignore': to not do anything at all
Parameters:mode (str) – mode of the warnings: determine what behaviour is chosen when a warning is set
